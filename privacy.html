Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our appointment booking form, we collect personal information you voluntarily provide including your name, date of birth, mobile number, location, and reason for visit. This information is transmitted via WhatsApp to Dr. Mohammed Arman's practice for the purpose of scheduling your appointment.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to contact you about and confirm your medical appointment with Dr. Mohammed Arman. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

3. Data Storage

This website does not use any backend server or database. Appointment information is communicated directly via WhatsApp. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

4. Cookies

This website may use minimal cookies for analytics purposes. No personal data is collected through cookies without your knowledge.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps for location display and WhatsApp for appointment communication. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.
