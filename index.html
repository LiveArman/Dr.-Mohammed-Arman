About The Doctor

Meet Dr. Mohammed Arman

Dr. Mohammed Arman is a highly qualified Physical Medicine, Pain Management & Rehabilitation Specialist (Physiatrist) based in Chittagong, Bangladesh. He completed his MBBS from Chittagong Medical College (CMC) and earned his FCPS in Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation in 2024.

He is an EULAR Certified Rheumatologist from Switzerland, trained in Neurorehabilitation under ISPRM (International Society of Physical & Rehabilitation Medicine), holds a Fellowship in Pain Management from India, and is certified in MSK Ultrasound (EULAR) — making him one of the most internationally credentialed physiatrists in the region.

Currently serving as a Consultant at Chittagong International Medical College & Hospital (CIMCH), Dr. Arman brings world-class expertise to his patients at Parkview Hospital and Qrex Diagnostic & Consultation Centre.
